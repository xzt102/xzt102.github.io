Xulong Tang

Associate Professor · Department of Computer Science · University of Pittsburgh

Welcome! I am an associate professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Pittsburgh. My research spans computer architecture and systems, with a focus on multi-GPU architectures, high-performance LLM infrastructure and systems, edge AI, and quantum systems. I received my Ph.D. from Penn State and Master from USTC.

Contact: 210 S. Bouquet Street, SENSQ 6115. Department of Computer Science, University of Pittsburgh, Pittsburgh, PA 15213
Email: tax6 AT pitt DOT edu

Curriculum Vitae Google Scholar DBLP LinkedIn
Prospective Students: I am looking for 2–3 self-motivated students to join my research group in Fall 2026. Potential research topics include: (i) multi-GPU infrastructure for LLMs, (ii) software–hardware co-design for edge AI, and (iii) fault-tolerant quantum compilers and systems. If you are interested, please send me a brief email along with your CV.
