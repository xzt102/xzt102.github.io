About

I am a member of Microsystems Design Lab (MDL) at Penn State, where work with my advisor Dr. Mahmut T. Kandemir. I am also collaborating with Dr. Chita R. Das and some brilliant graduate students in the High Performance Computing Lab.

My research interests lie in the area of both complier and computer architecture. More specifically, I am interested in performance and energy-efficient GPU platform design by exploring compiler and runtime system.

Research Experience

  • Dynamic parallelism optimization for irregular applications on GPGPUs.
  • Compiler-assisted optimization on manycore platforms. 2014 - Present.
  • Understanding CPU-GPU co-run degradations on integrated heterogeneous processors. 2014.
  • Built a two-layer video codec benchmark suite. Modified x264 codec into a fine-grain pipelined version to achieve task-level parallelism. 2011 - 2013.
  • Characterized program phases using Rodinia benchmark suite. 2010 - 2011.
