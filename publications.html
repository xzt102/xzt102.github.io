Xulong Tang

 

Welcome! I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Pittsburgh. I received my Ph.D. from Penn State, where I worked with my advisors Dr. Mahmut T. Kandemir and Dr. Chita R. Das on high-performance computing and advanced computer architectures. I received my M.S. in School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China. Before M.S., I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology, China.
