Xulong Tang

 

Welcome! I am an associate professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Pittsburgh. My research spans computer architecture and systems, with a focus on multi-GPU architectures, high-performance LLM infrastructure and systems, edge AI, and quantum systems. I received my Ph.D. from Penn State and Master from USTC.
